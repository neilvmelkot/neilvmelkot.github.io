Divvy Bike Sharing Analysis

A comprehensive study of Chicago’s Divvy program, merging trip and weather data to forecast demand and inform fleet management.

Highlights:

  • Integrated 2021–2024 Divvy trip data with granular NOAA weather logs; cleaned and filtered 21.6 million rides over 1,461 days.
  • Engineered temporal & weather features (hour, weekday, month, season, temperature, humidity, wind, precipitation).
  • Conducted exploratory analytics: continuous-weather impact plots, spatial heatmaps, and monthly trend comparisons.
  • Built and compared predictive models—Linear Regression (R²≈0.49), Random Forest (R²≈0.90), Gradient Boosting (R²≈0.87)—with 5‑fold cross-validation.
  • Developed interactive dashboards using Plotly and D3.js for actionable insights on bike‑sharing operations.
